About us

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multidisciplinary research university with multiple campuses across India. Amrita is ranked A++ by NAAC and is the 8th Ranked University in India as per the National Institution Ranking Framework (NIRF) for 2025, it also one of India's leading research universities. The institution is headquartered in Ettimadai, Tamil Nadu, and has campuses in Six Indian states: Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Haryana. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham organises a full-fledged campus placement campaign with MNCs and reputed companies' world over every year. 92% of students secured positions with 200 companies, attaining a highest CTC of ₹80.4 LPA and an average CTC of ₹9.08 LPA.

Coimbatore (Main Campus): A 400-acre campus featuring extensive infrastructure including a central library, hostels, swimming pool, and playgrounds. It hosts the School of Engineering, Business, Social Work, and Communication. Amaravati Campus: A 100-acre green campus in the new capital city of Andhra Pradesh, featuring modern laboratories and a focus on sustainability and technology. Bengaluru Campus: A 50-acre, tech-focused campus providing undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Engineering and Business. Amritapuri Campus: Located near the scenic, quiet village of Vallikavu, this campus blends science with traditional spirituality. Other Locations: Include Kochi, Chennai, Mysuru, Nagercoil, and a new campus in NCR Delhi (Faridabad).
